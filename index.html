Our Story At HOBY, we are passionate about delivering the purest and most natural honey straight from the pristine high mountain villages of Imereti and Racha regions in Georgia. Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the age-old art of beekeeping and a commitment to preserving the rich tradition of honey production in this beautiful corner of the world. Our honey is a testament to the lush landscapes and unspoiled environments of Georgia. The bees in our region feast on a diverse array of wildflowers, resulting in honey that is as diverse and flavorful as the Georgian terrain itself. We take pride in offering two distinctive packaging options: classic glass jars that reflect the artisanal quality of our honey and convenient spout pouches for those on the go. Quality and sustainability are at the core of everything we do. We work closely with local beekeepers who share our dedication to ethical and sustainable beekeeping practices. Our commitment to preserving the environment and supporting the livelihoods of local communities is unwavering.
